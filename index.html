The 1st Edition of The Biggest AI Conference in Algeria

AI in Ageria Summit is a 2-day premier event that will bring together the brightest minds and internationally-recognized experts in Artificial Intelligence from Algeria and around the world. The conference will feature a mix of keynotes by renowned speakers, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities. With a focus on AI including generative models and Large Language Models (LLMs), this event offers a platform to explore the latest advancements, applications, and integration of AI technologies. Attendees will gain insights into cutting-edge research, best practices, and strategies for leveraging AI, fostering AI education, and driving innovation in this rapidly evolving landscape. The event will be held at the Méridien Oran Hotel & Convention Centre in Oran, Algeria.
